The six dimensions

Rodney.AI's FS AI maturity model evaluates your organisation across six dimensions, each scored 1 (Initial) to 5 (Leading). The composite score reveals whether you are an AI laggard, follower, fast-follower, leader or differentiator.

1Strategy & Vision

Board-endorsed AI ambition and value pools.

2Governance & Risk

Policies, committees, three-lines model.

3Data & Platform

Data foundations, MLOps, GenAI tooling.

4Talent & Culture

AI literacy, hiring, change capacity.

5Use Cases & Value

Pipeline, scaling, measured ROI.

6Responsible AI

Ethics, fairness, regulatory readiness.
