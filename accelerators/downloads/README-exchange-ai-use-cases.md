## Sample Use Case: Smart Order Routing

**Description**  
AI system automatically routes orders to trading venues based on real-time market conditions, liquidity, and execution costs.

**Business Impact**  
$500K-2M annual savings through:
- Reduced execution costs (5-10 basis points)
- Faster order fills
- Better filled prices
- Reduced market impact

**Implementation Timeline**  
6-9 months from start to production

**Data Requirements**  
- Order flow history (3-5 years)
- Venue prices and liquidity (real-time)
- Latency and connectivity data
- Counterparty performance

**Key Success Metrics**  
- % of orders filled at best price
- Average execution time
- Cost per trade (bps)
- Market impact (reduction vs. baseline)

**ROI Example**  
Year 1: $500K investment, $750K savings = 50% year 1 ROI
Year 2: $100K operating cost, $1.2M savings = 11x ROI
3-Year NPV: $2M+

## Implementation Roadmap

**Phase 1: Pilot (3-4 months)**
- Select market segment or instrument
- Build proof of concept
- Backtest extensively
- Paper trade results
- Monitor and refine

**Phase 2: Limited Production (2-3 months)**
- Deploy to 10-20% of volume
- Real money with risk limits
- Monitor performance and risks
- Gather feedback and refine
- Establish incident procedures

**Phase 3: Full Deployment (1-2 months)**
- Scale to 100% of target volume
- Transition to operational team
- Establish ongoing monitoring
- Begin continuous optimization

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Data quality** - Good historical data essential for development
2. **Governance** - Clear approval and oversight procedures
3. **Risk controls** - Position limits, performance monitoring
4. **Team expertise** - Data science + domain knowledge
5. **Vendor selection** - Choose proven technology partner
6. **Regulatory alignment** - Compliance review in parallel
7. **Change management** - Trader and operations team buy-in
