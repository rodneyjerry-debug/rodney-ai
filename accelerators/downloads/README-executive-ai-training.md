# Executive AI Training Curriculum

**Document ID:** executive-ai-training.pdf  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** April 2025

## Overview

A complete executive education program designed for C-suite leaders and board members. Delivers AI literacy, decision-making frameworks, and strategic guidance without requiring technical expertise. Can be delivered in-person or virtually.

## What's Included

- **5-Module Curriculum**: Comprehensive coverage of AI fundamentals through strategy
- **Learning Objectives**: Clear outcomes and competencies for each module
- **Module Outlines**: Time-boxed, interactive sessions with discussion points
- **Assessment Rubrics**: Scoring guide for knowledge and application
- **Delivery Models**: Options for in-person and virtual formats
- **Success Metrics**: How to measure program effectiveness

## Target Audience

- Chief Executive Officer
- Chief Financial Officer
- Chief Operating Officer
- Chief Risk Officer
- Board Members
- Board Committee Chairs

## Program Format

- **Duration**: 10 hours total (5 modules × 2 hours each)
- **Delivery**: 5 weeks (1 session/week) or intensive 1-week program
- **Group Size**: 8-15 participants optimal
- **Facilitator**: Experienced AI executive or consultant
- **Prerequisite**: Executive curiosity; no technical knowledge required

## Module Breakdown

### Module 1: AI Fundamentals & Business Impact (2 hours)
**Learning Objectives**
- Explain AI vs. traditional software in business terms
- Understand AI capabilities and realistic limitations
- Identify AI opportunities relevant to your industry
- Build basic understanding of AI economics

**Topics Covered**
- What is AI? (Not science fiction, realistic expectations)
- Machine learning, deep learning, generative AI
- AI capabilities and limitations
- Use cases by industry and business function
- AI economics and ROI basics
- Discussion: Your industry opportunities

### Module 2: Building AI Business Cases (2 hours)
**Learning Objectives**
- Evaluate AI opportunities systematically
- Build financial models and ROI projections
- Assess risks and success factors
- Present compelling business cases

**Topics Covered**
- Use case evaluation framework
- Financial impact modeling (cost savings, revenue uplift)
- Risk assessment for AI projects
- Case studies: Successful implementations
- Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
- Workshop: Evaluate your business cases

### Module 3: Risk, Governance & Ethics (2 hours)
**Learning Objectives**
- Understand critical AI risks
- Establish appropriate governance
- Navigate ethical considerations
- Ensure regulatory compliance

**Topics Covered**
- AI risk landscape (model, data, operational, regulatory)
- Governance frameworks and oversight structures
- Bias, fairness, and ethical AI
- Regulatory requirements (EU AI Act, SR 11-7, etc.)
- Case studies: What can go wrong
- Governance decisions for your organization

### Module 4: Organizational Implementation (2 hours)
**Learning Objectives**
- Assess organizational AI readiness
- Plan capability development
- Manage change and adoption
- Execute implementation roadmap

**Topics Covered**
- Building organizational AI capability
- Change management for AI adoption
- Talent management (hiring, training, retention)
- Technology architecture decisions
- Vendor selection and management
- Workshop: Design your AI roadmap

### Module 5: Strategic AI Leadership (2 hours)
**Learning Objectives**
- Position AI as competitive advantage
- Guide board-level strategy discussions
- Understand industry trends and disruption
- Future-proof your strategy

**Topics Covered**
- AI as competitive differentiator
- Board-level AI governance
- Industry-specific AI trends
- Disruption and future scenarios
- Building AI-first organizational culture
- Capstone: Present your AI strategy

## Pre-Program Preparation

**For Facilitators**
- Review participant backgrounds and priorities
- Customize examples to your industry
- Prepare data and analytics relevant to organization
- Arrange guest speakers (AI practitioners)

**For Participants**
- Read "AI Fundamentals" brief (15 minutes)
- Complete pre-assessment survey (10 minutes)
- Identify 2-3 AI opportunities in advance (optional)

## Assessment & Measurement

**Knowledge Assessment**
- Quizzes after each module (5-10 questions)
- Final comprehensive assessment
- Target: 80%+ accuracy by end of program

**Application Assessment**
- Capstone presentation of AI strategy
- Business case development
- Governance framework design
- Rubric: Mastery (A), Proficient (B), Developing (C)

**Program Effectiveness Measurement**
- Pre/post program confidence assessment
- Participant satisfaction survey
- Follow-up on implementations (30/60/90 days)
- Measurable outcomes (strategy approval, initiatives launched)

## Delivery Models

### Option 1: In-Person Workshop (5 consecutive days)
- **Format**: Full-day sessions (8:30am-4:30pm) with breaks
- **Location**: Off-site or corporate facility
- **Group**: 8-15 executives
- **Cost**: $50K-$100K
- **Timeline**: 1-2 weeks
- **Advantage**: Immersive, networking, focused time

### Option 2: Weekly Virtual Program (5 weeks)
- **Format**: 2-hour sessions, same time weekly
- **Format**: Live facilitation with recorded sessions
- **Group**: Flexible (8-25+)
- **Cost**: $30K-$60K
- **Timeline**: 5 consecutive weeks
- **Advantage**: Less time commitment, flexible scheduling

### Option 3: Blended Format
- **Format**: 3 in-person days + 2 virtual sessions
- **Timing**: Spread over 6-8 weeks
- **Advantage**: Balance of focus and flexibility
