## Key Cost Drivers

**Token Volume**: Primary cost driver - millions of tokens processed  
**Model Selection**: GPT-4 costs 50-100x more than GPT-3.5  
**Output Length**: Completion tokens cost 2-4x more than prompt tokens  
**Infrastructure**: GPU compute, vector databases, storage  
**Operations**: Data prep, monitoring, fine-tuning, support

## Budget Template Approach

1. **Estimate monthly token volume** for each use case
2. **Calculate LLM costs** based on token pricing
3. **Add infrastructure costs** (compute, storage, networking)
4. **Include operational overhead** (monitoring, support, data prep)
5. **Apply optimization factors** (discounts, reserved capacity)
6. **Build quarterly and annual forecasts**

## Cost Optimization Techniques

**Prompt Optimization**: 5-20% savings through template engineering  
**Caching**: 10-30% savings by caching repeated instructions  
**Batching**: 15-25% savings through request aggregation  
**Model Downgrading**: 50-80% savings using smaller models  
**Reserved Capacity**: 20-40% savings with annual commitments  
**Spot Instances**: 60-80% savings for non-critical workloads  

## Financial Forecasting

Multi-year cost models should account for:
- **Volume growth** (typically 20-50% annually)
- **Model improvements** (newer models may be more efficient)
- **Pricing changes** (LLM costs historically declining 10-30% annually)
- **Optimization improvements** (additional 10-20% from ongoing tuning)

## Cost Benchmarking

Compare your projected costs against:
- **Peer organizations** (FS firms typically $100K-$500K annually)
- **Industry standards** (varies 10x by use case)
- **Your budget** (aim for 50-75% savings vs. manual process cost)

## Cost Management Best Practices

1. **Set cost budgets** by business unit and use case
2. **Monitor actual spend** weekly vs. monthly budget
3. **Establish optimization targets** (e.g., 20% reduction)
4. **Review token efficiency** (cost per transaction)
5. **Regular vendor evaluation** for best pricing
6. **Implement cost allocation** to business units
7. **Quarterly business case reviews** for ROI validation
