A professional, client-ready PowerPoint template for presenting AI strategy to financial services boards. Designed for Rodney.AI advisory practice.

This 12-slide template provides a comprehensive structure for board-level AI strategy briefings, covering opportunity sizing, governance, risk management, and recommended action plans. Built with Rodney.AI branding and premium design sensibilities.

## Tips for Effective Board Presentations

### Structure & Narrative
1. **Open with context** — Why this matters now (competitive, regulatory, strategic)
2. **Show current state honestly** — Board appreciates transparency
3. **Present clear opportunity** — Quantified, financial case
4. **Address risks upfront** — Show governance and controls in place
5. **Propose specific actions** — What decisions are needed? By when?
6. **Close with next steps** — Clear call to action and contact

### Delivery
- **Pace:** ~5 minutes per slide, allow 15-20 minutes for discussion
- **Tone:** Professional, confident, data-driven (not evangelical)
- **Emphasis:** Focus on business outcomes, not technology
- **Engagement:** Expect board members to have detailed questions on ROI, talent, risk

### Handling Objections
- **"This is too expensive"** → Show 18-month payback, 280% 3-year ROI
- **"We don't have AI talent"** → CoE hiring plan, vendor partners, training
- **"What about risk?"** → Governance structure, regulatory roadmap
- **"Why now?"** → Peer benchmarking, competitive urgency, strategic alignment
